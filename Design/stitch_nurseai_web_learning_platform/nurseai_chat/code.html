Can you explain the key nursing considerations for a patient starting on Warfarin?

person
bolt

Certainly. Warfarin is a critical anticoagulant with specific monitoring requirements. Here is a summary card of the key considerations:

Drug Profile: Warfarin (Coumadin)

High Alert
MECHANISM OF ACTION

Vitamin K antagonist; interferes with hepatic synthesis of clotting factors II, VII, IX, and X.

NURSING CONSIDERATIONS
  • Monitoring: Check PT/INR daily until stable, then every 1–4 weeks (Therapeutic INR: 2.0–3.0).
  • Antidote: Ensure Vitamin K (Phytonadione) is available in case of overdose.
  • Bleeding Precautions: Monitor for hematuria, melena, or ecchymosis.
  • Dietary Education: Advise consistent intake of leafy greens (Vitamin K rich foods).
INR TARGET 2.5
